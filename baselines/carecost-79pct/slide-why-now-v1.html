Why This Problem Exists Now

📋 The Regulatory Gap
CMS April 2026 enforcement arrived. Hospitals face up to $2M/year in fines for inaccurate price files. This turns compliance data into a real source we can build on.
💊 The Hidden Bills
A colonoscopy isn't one bill; it's four: physician fee, facility fee, anesthesia, and pathology. Every existing player lives on one track, but none bridge them all.
🤖 The AI Gap
AI symptom checkers (Ada Health, K Health) route to appointments but show no costs. We bridge the clinical and financial data domains to solve the trust deficit.
