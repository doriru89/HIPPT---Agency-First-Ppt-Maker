BUSINESS MODEL

Free government data. Near-zero marginal cost. Four revenue streams.

GoodRx built $797M/yr on free prescription data with ~90% gross margins. CareCost applies the same structural model to 100+ TB of mandated procedure pricing -- a 10x larger market.

Revenue 1 -- B2B Provider
Compliance-as-a-Service
Automated Good Faith Estimates and CMS compliance documentation. Providers face $2M/yr fines for non-compliance starting April 2026.
$4-15 PMPM or per-estimate pricing
Revenue 2 -- Consumer DTC
Free Tier + Premium
Free cost search for everyone. Premium tier adds personalized out-of-pocket estimates with insurance-specific calculations.
Freemium conversion on high-intent users
Revenue 3 -- Data/API
Infrastructure Licensing
Cleaned, structured pricing data via API for navigation platforms, employer benefits tools, and health plan comparisons.
Per-query or annual license
